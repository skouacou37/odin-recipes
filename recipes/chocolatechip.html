Chocolatechip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 c all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 c brown sugar
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1 c chocolate chips
  • 8 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix It Up "Using your electric mixer, combine both of the sugars and the butter."
  2. Bring in the Vanilla "After the sugars and butter are mixed completely, turn the mixer on low and add the vanilla."
  3. Now Bring in the Egg "Add the egg into the mixture and mix until it's just combined, but don't over mix this step."
